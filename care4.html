Our Mission

Building healthy communities for all through quality, equitable, accessible healthcare.

Everyday, people throughout America struggle with choosing between paying for food and a place to live or going to the doctor and getting their needed medications. Free and Charitable Clinics and Pharmacies are on the frontlines making sure access to healthcare is not just a dream, but a reality.

Our Vision

A just society that ensures a healthy life and well-being for all.

Access to affordable healthcare is a crucial part of public health. At the NAFC, we believe that access to healthcare should be a right, not a privilege. We value human dignity and access to quality, compassionate and equitable healthcare.

Building a Healthy America, One Person at a Time.

Our Impact What We Do

The National Association of Free & Charitable Clinics (NAFC) is the only nonprofit 501c(3) organization whose mission is solely focused on the issues and needs of medically underserved people throughout the U.S. and the more than 1,400 Free and Charitable Clinics and Charitable Pharmacies that serve them. NAFC programs provide a wide range of support to our members and in turn patients through funding, education and training, advocacy, standards, disaster relief, and more.
