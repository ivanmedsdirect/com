Ivan Care Foundation

Ivan Care Foundation's mission is solely focused on the issues and needs of the medically underserved throughout the nation and the more than 1,400 Free and Charitable Clinics and Charitable Pharmacies that serve them. We provide a wide range of support to our members and in turn patients through funding, education and training, advocacy, standards, disaster relief, and more.

Examples of services that are found at Free & Charitable Clinics include Primary Medical Care, Chronic Disease Care and Management, Dental Care, Behavioral/Mental Health Care, Vision and other Specialty Care, Care Coordination, Womenâ€™s Health Care, Health Education, Smoking Cessation, Health Navigation, Immunizations, Pharmacy Services/Medication Access.

Our Mission

Building healthy communities for all through quality, equitable, accessible healthcare.

Everyday, people throughout America struggle with choosing between paying for food and a place to live or going to the doctor and getting their needed medications. Free and Charitable Clinics and Pharmacies are on the frontlines making sure access to healthcare is not just a dream, but a reality.

Our Vision

A just society that ensures a healthy life and well-being for all.

Access to affordable healthcare is a crucial part of public health. At the Ivan's Care Foundation, we believe that access to healthcare should be a right, not a privilege. We value human dignity and access to quality, compassionate and equitable healthcare.
